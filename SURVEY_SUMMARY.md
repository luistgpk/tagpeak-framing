# TagPeak Framing Study - Survey Summary

## Survey Intention & Purpose

This survey is designed to study **email framing effects on consumer behavior** regarding the TagPeak benefit platform. The research investigates how different email message framings (financial markets frame, cashback/consumption frame, and generic reward frame) influence:

- Consumer involvement with promotional benefits
- Message processing and engagement
- Behavioral intentions to use the platform
- Perceptions of ease of use, clarity, and advantage
- Willingness to adopt the benefit

The study uses an **A/B/C testing framework** where participants are randomly assigned to one of three email framing conditions, allowing researchers to compare the effectiveness of different communication strategies.

**Duration**: Approximately 7-10 minutes  
**Languages**: English, Spanish, Portuguese  
**Data Collection**: Anonymous and confidential

---

## Survey Flow & Structure

The survey follows a structured multi-screen flow:

1. **Language Selection**
2. **Welcome Screen**
3. **Demographics**
4. **Investment Involvement** (baseline measure)
5. **Financial Literacy Assessment**
6. **Initial Involvement** (with promotional benefits)
7. **Brand Selection**
8. **Email Notification**
9. **Email Framing** (Condition A, B, or C - randomized)
10. **Exclusion Questions**
11. **Manipulation Check**
12. **Message Involvement**
13. **Intention to Use** (before website)
14. **Tagpeak Information**
15. **Website View** (embedded Tagpeak.com)
16. **Intention to Use** (after website)
17. **Ease of Use Evaluation**
18. **Clarity & Advantage Evaluation**
19. **Concerns**
20. **Thank You**

### 4. Investment Involvement Screen
**Purpose**: Measure baseline involvement with investment products (before exposure to Tagpeak)

**Statement**: *"For me, investments (such as ETFs, stocks, bonds, etc.) are:"*

**Questions** (7-point Likert scale, some items reversed):

1. **Important** ↔ **Not Important** (reversed)
2. **Relevant** ↔ **Irrelevant** (reversed)
3. **Mean Nothing** ↔ **Mean a Lot to Me**
4. **Worthless** ↔ **Valuable**

---

### 5. Financial Literacy Screen
**Purpose**: Assess participants' financial knowledge and literacy

**Questions**:

1. **Compound Interest Question**
   - *"Suppose you have €100 in an account that earns 2% per year. After 5 years, how much will you have in the account?"*
   - Options:
     - More than €102
     - Exactly €102
     - Less than €102
     - Don't know

2. **Inflation Question**
   - *"If your account interest rate is 1% per year and inflation is 2%, after one year the money allows you to buy:"*
   - Options:
     - More
     - The same
     - Less
     - Don't know

3. **Diversification Question**
   - *"Buying shares of a single company is normally safer than investing in a diversified fund."*
   - Options:
     - True
     - False
     - Don't know

### 9. Email Framing Screen
**Purpose**: Present one of three email framing conditions (randomly assigned)

**Three Framing Conditions**:

#### **Condition A - Explicit Financial Frame**
- **Subject**: "Want to link your purchases to financial markets?"
- **Focus**: Emphasizes connection to financial markets, investment-like language
- **Key Message**: Cashback linked to financial markets, managed by specialized team

#### **Condition B - Cashback/Consumption Frame**
- **Subject**: "Recover a portion of what you spend in our store"
- **Focus**: Emphasizes cashback and consumption benefits
- **Key Message**: Variable value cashback that can evolve over time

#### **Condition C - Generic Reward Frame**
- **Subject**: "We have a new reward for you"
- **Focus**: Generic reward language, minimal financial terminology
- **Key Message**: Free reward that can grow over time, additional benefit

**Email Content** (all conditions include):
- Greeting with participant's first name
- Introduction to Tagpeak partnership
- Explanation of the benefit
- Instructions to use code "TAGPEAK" at checkout
- Brand signature

### 10. Exclusion Questions Screen
**Purpose**: Verify participants read and understood the email (exclusion criteria)

**Questions**:

1. **Partner Name Recognition**
   - *"What is the name of the partner the email refers to?"*
   - Options:
     - Tagpeak
     - Squeak
     - TikTik
     - Moneypink

2. **Cost Understanding**
   - *"Is there any additional cost in the process?"*
   - Options:
     - Yes
     - No

---

### 11. Manipulation Check Screen
**Purpose**: Open-ended assessment of what participants thought about the benefit (manipulation check)

**Question**:
- *"After reading the email, what thoughts came to your mind about the benefit?"*

**Response Format**:
- Free text (minimum 30 characters required)

---

### 12. Message Involvement Screen
**Purpose**: Measure cognitive and emotional involvement with the email message

**Statement**: *"While reading the email, would you say that:"*

**Questions** (9-point Likert scale):

1. **Not Interested** ↔ **Very Interested**
2. **Not Absorbed** ↔ **Very Absorbed**
3. **Read the Message Quickly** ↔ **Read the Message with Attention**
4. **Irrelevant to You** ↔ **Relevant to You**
5. **Boring** ↔ **Interesting**
6. **Not Engaging** ↔ **Engaging**

---

### 13. Intention to Use Screen (Before Website)
**Purpose**: Measure behavioral intentions before exposure to the Tagpeak website

**Scenario**: *"Imagine you are about to make a purchase at [Selected Brand]."*

**Instruction**: *"Please indicate how likely you are to use this new platform on your next purchase, answering the questions below:"*

**Questions** (7-point Likert scale):

1. **Unlikely** ↔ **Likely**
2. **Impossible** ↔ **Possible**
3. **Definitely Would Not Use** ↔ **Definitely Would Use**
4. **Not Frequent** ↔ **Very Frequent**

---

### 14. Tagpeak Information Screen
**Purpose**: Prepare participants to view the Tagpeak website

**Content**:
- "You are now going to enter the Tagpeak website"
- "Browse the website freely and carefully to answer some questions afterwards."

---

### 15. Website View Screen
**Purpose**: Allow participants to explore the Tagpeak website

**Features**:
- Embedded iframe displaying https://tagpeak.com
- Browser-like interface
- Time tracking (records how long participants view the website)
- Note about potential security restrictions

---

### 16. Intention to Use Screen (After Website)
**Purpose**: Measure behavioral intentions after exposure to the Tagpeak website

**Scenario**: *"Now that you know Tagpeak better, imagine you are about to make a purchase at [Selected Brand]."*

**Instruction**: *"Please indicate how likely you are to use this new platform on your next purchase, answering the questions below:"*

**Questions** (7-point Likert scale - same as before website):

1. **Unlikely** ↔ **Likely**
2. **Impossible** ↔ **Possible**
3. **Definitely Would Not Use** ↔ **Definitely Would Use**
4. **Not Frequent** ↔ **Very Frequent**

---

### 17. Ease of Use Evaluation Screen
**Purpose**: Assess perceived ease of using the benefit

**Instruction**: *"According to the information presented previously, evaluate the following statements:"*

**Questions** (7-point Likert scale: Totally Disagree ↔ Totally Agree):

1. *"It is difficult to use the benefit"*
2. *"I believe it is easy to use the benefit"*

---

### 18. Clarity & Advantage Evaluation Screen
**Purpose**: Measure clarity of usage process and perceived advantage

**Instruction**: *"Evaluate the following statements:"*

**Questions** (7-point Likert scale: Totally Disagree ↔ Totally Agree):

1. *"The steps in the benefit usage process are clear to me"*
2. *"I feel secure about how to use the benefit effectively."*
3. *"This benefit seems more advantageous to me than other discount or cashback options I know."*
4. *"With this benefit, I feel I am in a better position than with traditional benefits."*

---

**Question**:

**Response Format**:
- Free text (minimum 30 characters required)

### 20. Thank You Screen

**Content**:

## Research Design Features

### Randomization
- Participants are randomly assigned to one of three email framing conditions (A, B, or C)
- Brand selection is participant choice (not randomized)

### Control Measures
- Exclusion questions verify email comprehension
- Manipulation check assesses attention and processing
- Baseline measures (investment involvement, initial involvement) collected before treatment

### Repeated Measures
- Intention to use measured both before and after website exposure
- Allows comparison of website impact on behavioral intentions

### Multi-Language Support
- Full translation in English, Spanish, and Portuguese
- Consistent question structure across languages
